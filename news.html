Recently Arrested Inmates!

July 7, 2019

If you still cannot find your inmate, or they are not in a prison but a jail you will want to search using a website called VINELink.

VINE stands for Victim Notification system, and it covers almost every state and will allow instant notification of a change to an offenders status.

You can choose to be notified if the inmate is moved, or released.

You can be notified via Text Message, Email, or Telephone.
